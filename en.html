About Lang Biang

Lang Biang is the highest mountain in Da Lat.
As you probably already know, we come from the city of Da Lat and Lang Biang is our symbol of ambition, humility, respect and peace.

Our restaurant is located next to the Albertov stop and is very accessible from the center.
We offer a really wide range of Vietnamese dishes.
We make them with love and authenticity, as we have learned from our parents.

Bún chả xiên

Bún chả xiên comes from Hanoi. Unlike the classic Bún chả, Bún chả xiên has a different aroma, as it is grilled on a stick and accompanied by the delicious aroma of grilled oak grilled meat.

Pad Thai tôm

When we say pad thai, every lover of Asian cuisine must immediately know what they are talking about. This Thai dish made of rice noodles is now almost legendary. Although in fact it is not even Thai - it got to the country only thanks to Chinese settlers. However, the Thais quickly made it one of the most popular national dishes.
